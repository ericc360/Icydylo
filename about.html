High School Life

Four years of showing up -
to hallways that felt endless,
to friends who made it feel smaller,
to a version of myself I'm still becoming.

Now the end is here,
and it feels like both -
the exhale I've been waiting for
and the breath I'm not ready to let go.

Social Life

Not a big crowd -
just a few who showed up
every time, without question,
and that was always enough.

We didn't need much-
just time, and each other.
Turns out that's the richest thing,
money neer could've brought it.

Small circle, much memories.

Personal Life

Music was the one thing
that always understood -
every feeling I couldn't say out loud
already had a melody.

And then there were the thoughts -
the ones that almost were,
realities that lived at the edge of maybe,
close enough to feel, too far to hold.

Music understood me when I couldn't. Some things almost were, and that's okay.
